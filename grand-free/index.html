About the Conference

AICTE – VAANI SPONSORED TWO DAYS NATIONAL CONFERENCE IN TAMIL LANGUAGE ON GREEN SOLUTIONS FOR ENERGY, ENVIRONMENT AND SUSTAINABILITY GSEES 2025
Organized by Department of Chemistry, University College of Engineering, Thiruchirappalli

Our Mission

To provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share and discuss the latest advancements in chemical engineering for sustainable processes.

Key Themes

Green chemistry, process intensification, renewable energy, waste management, and innovative technologies for sustainability in chemical engineering.

Who Should Attend?

Students, researchers, faculty, and professionals interested in chemical engineering, sustainability, and related fields.

Venue

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the National Conference on Recent Advances in Chemical Engineering for Process Sustainability.

How do I register for the conference?
You can register online through the "Register Now" button on this website or contact the organizing committee for assistance.
What is the last date for abstract submission?
The last date for abstract submission is 15th September 2025. Please refer to the brochure for detailed guidelines.
Will I receive a participation certificate?
Yes, all registered participants will receive certificates. Presenters will receive additional recognition.
Who can attend the conference?
The conference is open to students, researchers, faculty, and professionals in chemical engineering and related fields.
